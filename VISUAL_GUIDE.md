## Color Scheme

**Primary Colors:**
- Cyan/Blue: `#0ea5e9` - Technology, trust
- Purple: `#d946ef` - Creativity, AI
- Pink: `#c026d3` - Innovation

**Background:**
- Dark Slate: `#0f172a`, `#1e293b`, `#334155`

**Text:**
- White: `#ffffff` for headings
- Slate-300: `#cbd5e1` for body text
- Slate-400: `#94a3b8` for secondary text

---

## Animation Types

1. **Entrance Animations**: Fade-in + slide-up on scroll
2. **Background**: Floating gradient blobs
3. **Hover Effects**: Scale, glow, color transitions
4. **Waveforms**: Bars animate height on play
5. **Scroll Indicator**: Bouncing mouse icon

---

## Responsive Breakpoints

- **Mobile**: < 768px (single column, stacked)
- **Tablet**: 768px - 1024px (2 columns)
- **Desktop**: > 1024px (3 columns, full layout)

---

## Typography Scale

- **Hero H1**: 5xl-8xl (72px-96px)
- **Section H2**: 4xl-6xl (48px-72px)
- **Card H3**: 2xl (24px)
- **Body**: xl (18px-20px)
- **Small**: sm (14px)
